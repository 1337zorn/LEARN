So, who are they?

Technical Difficulties have been together since the mid 2000s. On the radio, they won the inaugural Kevin Greening Award for Creativity in 2008, and a Bronze for Best Entertainment the year after at the Student Radio Awards. they finished 2009 by making a three-part video series. Our reverse trivia quiz podcast followed, and after 33 episodes we morphed it into Citation Needed. After over 50 Episodes of Citation Needed, the bunch is on the look out for new format to entertain their dear viewers and so, The Experiments and Two of These People Are Lying has been born.
